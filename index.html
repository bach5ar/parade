July is National Ice Cream Month and this Sunday is National Ice Cream Day. These two unofficially-official holidays are bringing ice cold dessert lovers super sweet deals and, in some cases, free ice cream.

These frozen freebies are simply the cherry-on-top of a week that's been filled with fast food promotions. If you missed free fries on National Fry Day, National 7/11 Day's priceless slurpees, or complimentary mac and cheese on National Mac & Cheese Day, mark your calendars for July 17!

Whole Foods National Ice Cream Day Discounts

Now through July 19, Whole Foods' ice cream and frozen novelties are up to 25 percent off, and Amazon Prime members can receive an additional 10% off!

Select Whole Foods Markets will also be celebrating National Ice Cream Day with an exclusive Taco Sunday developed by Camila Alves McConaughey.

On July 17, from 1-4 p.m. local time, the first 500 customers at each participating Whole Foods will have the opportunity to try one for FREE! These locations include: the Whole Foods on W. Olive Ave. in Burbank, California; the 3rd St. location in Brooklyn, New York; and the Whole Foods on Lamar Blvd. in Austin, Texas.
